Attribution Models in Sales Processes

Understanding Attribution Models

Attribution models determine how credit for sales and conversions is assigned to touchpoints in the customer journey.

First Touch

Gives 100% credit to the first customer interaction

Pro: Simple to implement and understand
Con: Ignores all other touchpoints in the journey

Last Touch

Gives 100% credit to the final customer interaction before conversion

Pro: Highlights conversion-driving touchpoints
Con: Undervalues early nurturing touchpoints

Linear

Distributes credit equally across all touchpoints in the customer journey

Pro: Recognizes all touchpoints in the journey
Con: May overvalue less important interactions

Time Decay

Gives more credit to touchpoints closer to the conversion

Pro: Recognizes recency's importance
Con: May undervalue important early touches
